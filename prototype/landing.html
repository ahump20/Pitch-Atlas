Two things, every time

PROOF 01

The signature medium

An original-geometry interactive 3D ball that teaches the pitch. A pitch is defined by its spin axis, so the axis is authored and the Magnus force is computed from it, not hand-placed. It points up for the four-seam, down for the curve, leaned for the sinker, and runs short for the gyro slider whose spin does little work. On scroll the ball dissolves into a 2D schematic of the same seam-point function.

PROOF 02

Provenance on every number

Every visible figure carries a Source and a confidence. Anything that cannot be verified renders as approximate or unverified, with a note. A broken citation throws at build, so a dead source never reaches a visitor, and freshness comes from real metadata, never a typed-in string.
